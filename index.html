About Us

Royal Eats is a contemporary multi cuisine restaurant showcasing techniques rooted in different parts of the world, carefully sourcing only the best ingredients from only the best places in the world. Building off the vibrant food scene of different regions, Royal Eats displays an inspiring take on Indian, French, Chinese, Mexican, Mughlai and Italian cuisine in a relaxing environment. This enables our genuine approach to food & hospitality to shine.

Royal Eats was recognized with one star in the 2019 & 2020 Michelin guides.
